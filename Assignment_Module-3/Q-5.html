Q-5: What are different types of lists in HTML? With Example.


Ans:There are two type of lists,
        1. order lists - in which order are metter.
        2. unorder list- in which order are not metter.

For Ex-
  1. Order list
    1. Item 1
    2. Item 2
    3. Item 3
  2. Unorder list
    • Bike
    • Car
    • Bus
    • Truck
